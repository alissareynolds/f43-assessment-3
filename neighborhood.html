Welcome to Vintage Oaks

Vintage Oaks is a neighborhood that enjoys the escape from city life, hidden away in Weatherford Texas. This neighborhood is only 15 minutes away from Lake Weatherford, and 30 minutes away from Eagle Mountain Lake. You also get the added benefit of being close Downtown Forth Worth, where you can experience many activities and resturants.
